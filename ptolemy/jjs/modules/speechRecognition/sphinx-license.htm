License for Sphinx

Sphinx4 is a speech recognition engine developed at Carnegie Mellon University. PtolemyII includes an Accessor for speech recognition using sphinx4 at ptII/ptolemy/actor/lib/jjs/modules/speechRecognition

The Sphinx libraries are at ptII/lib/sphinx4-core-5prealpha.jar and ptII/lib/sphinx4-data-5prealpha .
